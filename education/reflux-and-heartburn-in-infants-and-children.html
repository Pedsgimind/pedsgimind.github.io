Reflux and heartburn in infants and children

Patient & family education. Updated January 2026.

What this is

Reflux is common in infants and often improves with time. Older children may feel chest or upper abdominal burning.

Care

  • Smaller meals
  • Upright positioning
  • Avoid late meals

When to worry

  • Poor weight gain
  • Blood in vomit
  • Breathing problems
