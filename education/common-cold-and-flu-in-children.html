Common cold and flu in children

Patient & family education. Updated January 2026. This information is for education only and does not replace individual medical advice.

Overview

Cough, runny nose, and fever are commonly viral. Most improve with supportive care.

What helps

  • Fluids, rest, and saline nose drops/spray.
  • Honey for cough in children over 1 year.
  • Cool-mist humidifier for comfort.

When to seek care

  • Breathing difficulty or fast breathing.
  • Persistent high fever or worsening after initial improvement.
  • Signs of dehydration.
