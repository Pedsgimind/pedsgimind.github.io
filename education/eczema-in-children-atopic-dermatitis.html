Eczema in children (atopic dermatitis)

Patient & family education. Updated January 2026. This information is for education only and does not replace individual medical advice.

Overview

Eczema is a chronic itchy skin condition that flares with dryness, infections, and irritants.

Daily care

  • Moisturize twice daily (thick cream/ointment).
  • Short lukewarm baths; gentle soap only where needed.
  • Use prescribed anti-inflammatory creams for flares.

When to seek care

  • Signs of infection (weeping, crusting, fever).
  • Severe sleep disruption or uncontrolled itch.
