Accident and injury prevention for families

Patient & family education. Updated January 2026. This information is for education only and does not replace individual medical advice.

Why it matters

Injuries are a leading cause of preventable harm in children. Small changes at home and in cars reduce risk.

Key safety points

  • Car seats/booster seats appropriate for age and size.
  • Helmet use for biking/scooters.
  • Safe storage of medicines and chemicals.
  • Water safety and supervision.

Seek urgent care if

  • Head injury with vomiting, confusion, or worsening headache.
  • Breathing difficulty after choking or ingestion.
