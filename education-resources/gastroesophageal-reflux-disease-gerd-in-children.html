Gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD) in children

Patient & family education. Updated January 2026.

Overview

GERD occurs when reflux causes ongoing symptoms or complications.

Evaluation

  • Growth review
  • Symptom assessment
  • Tests if symptoms persist

Treatment

  • Lifestyle changes
  • Medication if advised
