Feeding Difficulty / Picky Eating

Practical routines that reduce mealtime battles and support nutrition.

Helpful principles

  • Parents choose what and when; child chooses how much.
  • Offer structured meals/snacks; avoid grazing all day.
  • Keep mealtimes calm and time-limited (20–30 minutes).

What to try

  • Serve 1–2 “safe foods” with each meal plus one new food.
  • Repeat exposure (10–15 times) before deciding a child “doesn’t like” a food.
  • Limit distractions (screens) during meals.

When to seek help

  • Poor growth or weight loss.
  • Choking, gagging, vomiting with feeds.
  • Very restricted diet (few foods only) or high anxiety at meals.
