Anemia in children

Patient & family education. Updated January 2026. This information is for education only and does not replace individual medical advice.

Overview

Anemia means a low red blood cell level. Iron deficiency is the most common cause.

Symptoms

  • Fatigue, pale skin, reduced exercise tolerance.
  • Poor appetite or pica (craving non-food items).

Evaluation and treatment

  • Blood tests to confirm the cause.
  • Iron supplementation when indicated, plus dietary iron.
  • Follow-up testing to ensure recovery.

Seek urgent care if

  • Fainting, chest pain, or severe weakness.
  • Rapidly worsening symptoms.
