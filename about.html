About Dr. Mohammad Hussein

Dr. Mohammad Hussein is a Royal College–certified pediatrician and fellowship-trained pediatric gastroenterologist with a special interest in endoscopy, nutrition and chronic digestive disorders in children.

Clinical Interests

  • Inflammatory bowel disease (Crohn disease, ulcerative colitis)
  • Celiac disease and nutrition-related disorders
  • Chronic abdominal pain, constipation and functional GI disorders
  • Reflux, feeding problems, and failure to thrive
  • Pediatric endoscopy, colonoscopy and polyp management

Philosophy of Care

Dr. Hussein believes that children and families deserve clear explanations, honest discussion of options and a shared plan that respects family preferences, culture and circumstances. Each visit includes time for questions and for reviewing test results in understandable language.
