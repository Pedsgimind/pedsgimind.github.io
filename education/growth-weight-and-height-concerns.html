Growth, weight, and height concerns

Patient & family education. Updated January 2026. This information is for education only and does not replace individual medical advice.

Overview

Growth is best assessed by trends over time rather than a single measurement.

Common reasons for concern

  • Dropping percentiles over time.
  • Weight gain without height gain (or the reverse).
  • Symptoms suggesting poor intake or malabsorption.

What clinicians review

  • Growth charts, diet, sleep, activity, and family height patterns.
  • Targeted labs if needed.
