Screen time guidance for families

Patient & family education. Updated January 2026.

What this is

Screen time includes TVs, phones, tablets, computers, and gaming devices. Balanced screen use supports healthy sleep, behavior, learning, and digestion.

Why it matters

  • Poor sleep and delayed bedtime.
  • Increased snacking and obesity risk.
  • Behavior and attention difficulties.
  • Worsening functional abdominal pain.

Healthy limits

  • Under 18 months: avoid screens except video chat.
  • Ages 2–5: about 1 hour/day of high-quality content.
  • Ages 6+: consistent limits protecting sleep and activity.

When to seek help

  • Severe sleep or behavioral problems.
  • Screen use interfering with eating or safety.
