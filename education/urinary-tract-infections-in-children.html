Urinary tract infections (UTIs) in children

Patient & family education. Updated January 2026. This information is for education only and does not replace individual medical advice.

Overview

UTIs can cause fever, painful urination, belly/back pain, or accidents.

Why early evaluation matters

  • Untreated UTIs can spread to the kidneys.
  • Testing urine is important, especially in febrile infants.

Seek urgent care if

  • High fever with lethargy or vomiting.
  • Back pain, dehydration, or very young infant with fever.
