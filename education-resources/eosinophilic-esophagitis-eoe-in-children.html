Eosinophilic esophagitis (EoE) in children

Patient & family education. Updated January 2026.

Overview

EoE is a chronic immune-mediated condition causing inflammation of the esophagus.

Symptoms

  • Feeding refusal
  • Difficulty swallowing
  • Food getting stuck

Management

  • Dietary elimination
  • Topical swallowed steroids
