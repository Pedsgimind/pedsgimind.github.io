Clinic & Consultation Services

We provide comprehensive evaluation and follow-up for infants, children and adolescents with digestive, liver and nutrition problems.

New Consultations

  • Abdominal pain, nausea, vomiting and reflux
  • Constipation, encopresis and stool withholding
  • Chronic diarrhea, blood in stool, weight loss
  • Feeding difficulties and poor growth
  • Abnormal blood work (anemia, liver tests, inflammation)

Endoscopy & Procedures

Depending on local hospital and endoscopy-suite arrangements, we may be able to provide:

  • Upper endoscopy (gastroscopy)
  • Colonoscopy and polyp removal
  • Biopsy for celiac disease, IBD and other conditions
  • Therapeutic procedures as indicated

Procedures are performed in a hospital or accredited endoscopy unit with pediatric-appropriate sedation or anesthesia.

Nutrition & Growth

  • Growth faltering and underweight children
  • Dietary management of celiac disease and food intolerance
  • Enteral feeding (NG, G-tube) planning in collaboration with hospital teams
  • Refeeding risk assessment and monitoring

General Pediatrics

When appropriate, we also support general pediatric care such as preventive counseling, simple acute illnesses, and coordination of complex chronic conditions in children followed primarily for GI issues.
