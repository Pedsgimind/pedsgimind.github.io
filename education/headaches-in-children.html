Headaches in children

Patient & family education. Updated January 2026. This information is for education only and does not replace individual medical advice.

Overview

Most headaches in children are benign and related to sleep, hydration, stress, or migraines.

Helpful steps

  • Regular sleep and meals.
  • Hydration and limiting caffeine.
  • Headache diary (triggers, timing, severity).

Red flags

  • Headache that wakes from sleep, morning vomiting, neurological symptoms.
  • Sudden “worst headache” or severe stiff neck.
