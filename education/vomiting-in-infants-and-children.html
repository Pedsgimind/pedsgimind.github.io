Vomiting in infants and children

Patient & family education. Updated January 2026.

Overview

Vomiting is common in children and often related to infections, reflux, or feeding intolerance.

Common causes

  • Viral gastroenteritis
  • Reflux
  • Food intolerance
  • Motion sickness

Home care

  • Small frequent fluids
  • Oral rehydration solutions
  • Avoid force-feeding

Urgent signs

  • Green or bloody vomit
  • Severe abdominal pain
  • Lethargy or dehydration
