Asthma in children: a family guide

Patient & family education. Updated January 2026. This information is for education only and does not replace individual medical advice.

Overview

Asthma is airway inflammation causing cough, wheeze, and shortness of breath. Many children have symptoms mainly with colds or exercise.

Key parts of control

  • Trigger reduction (smoke exposure is a major trigger).
  • Reliever inhaler for symptoms.
  • Controller medicine for frequent symptoms (as advised).

Go to urgent care if

  • Breathing is hard, child cannot speak in full sentences, or lips look blue.
  • Reliever inhaler is not helping as expected.
