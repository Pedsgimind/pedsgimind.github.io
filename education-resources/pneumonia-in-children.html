Pneumonia in children

Patient & family education. Updated January 2026. This information is for education only and does not replace individual medical advice.

Overview

Pneumonia is infection of the lungs. It can be viral or bacterial.

Symptoms

  • Fever and cough.
  • Fast breathing, chest pain, or low energy.

When to seek care

  • Breathing difficulty, persistent fast breathing, or dehydration.
  • Worsening symptoms after several days.
