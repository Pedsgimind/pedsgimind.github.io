Migraine in children

Patient & family education. Updated January 2026. This information is for education only and does not replace individual medical advice.

Overview

Migraines can cause pounding head pain, nausea, light sensitivity, and sometimes abdominal symptoms.

Prevention

  • Regular sleep, meals, and hydration.
  • Identify triggers (stress, skipped meals, dehydration).

When to seek care

  • New neurological symptoms (weakness, vision loss).
  • Severe or changing pattern.
