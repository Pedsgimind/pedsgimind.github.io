Common Pediatric GI Conditions

Below is a sample of the conditions frequently assessed and followed in clinic. This list is not complete; if your child has a digestive or liver concern, feel free to ask if we can help.

Esophagus & Stomach

  • Gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD)
  • Recurrent vomiting and feeding refusal
  • Eosinophilic esophagitis
  • Helicobacter pylori infection

Intestine & Colon

  • Constipation and stool withholding
  • Functional abdominal pain and irritable bowel syndrome
  • Chronic diarrhea and malabsorption
  • Inflammatory bowel disease (Crohn disease & ulcerative colitis)

Liver & Pancreas

  • Elevated liver enzymes
  • Chronic hepatitis and autoimmune liver disease
  • Nonalcoholic fatty liver disease
  • Recurrent pancreatitis

Nutrition & Growth

  • Celiac disease
  • Failure to thrive / poor weight gain
  • Feeding difficulties and selective eating
  • Micronutrient deficiencies (iron, vitamin D, etc.)
