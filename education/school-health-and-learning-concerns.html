School health and learning concerns

Patient & family education. Updated January 2026. This information is for education only and does not replace individual medical advice.

Overview

School challenges can be linked to sleep, vision/hearing, attention, anxiety, or chronic medical symptoms (including abdominal pain).

Helpful steps

  • Speak with teachers and track patterns.
  • Consider vision/hearing checks.
  • Ask about learning supports if needed.

Seek help promptly if

  • Child refuses school due to significant anxiety or pain.
  • There are safety concerns or severe mood symptoms.
