Chronic diarrhea in children

Patient & family education. Updated January 2026.

Overview

Diarrhea lasting more than 2–3 weeks may have dietary, infectious, or inflammatory causes.

Evaluation

  • Growth and diet review
  • Stool tests if needed
  • Blood tests in selected cases

When to worry

  • Poor weight gain
  • Blood in stool
  • Night-time diarrhea
