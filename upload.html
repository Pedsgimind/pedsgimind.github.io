Secure Medical Records Upload

Use this secure link to upload medical records (labs, imaging reports, PDFs, discharge summaries, clinic notes). Do not upload urgent/emergency concerns.

Important:
  • Only upload records for the purpose of clinical review/telemedicine coordination.
  • Include the patient’s full name and date of birth in the file name (or inside the document).
  • Avoid sending passwords in the same message as the files.
Open Secure Upload Portal

What to upload

  • Lab results (PDF).
  • Imaging reports (PDF).
  • Endoscopy/pathology reports (PDF).
  • Hospital discharge summaries (PDF).
  • Prior clinic letters/consult notes (PDF).

If you cannot upload

You can email administrative inquiries to the clinic, or contact Dr. Hussein directly for records inquiries (as listed on the website).
