Constipation in children

Patient & family education. Updated January 2026.

Overview

Constipation causes hard, painful, or infrequent stools. It is common and usually functional.

Symptoms

  • Hard stools
  • Pain with stooling
  • Soiling accidents

Management

  • Regular toilet sitting
  • Adequate fluids and fiber
  • Stool softeners as prescribed

Urgent signs

  • Vomiting
  • Weight loss
  • Blood mixed in stool
