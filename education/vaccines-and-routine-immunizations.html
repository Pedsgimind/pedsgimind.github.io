Vaccines and routine immunizations

Patient & family education. Updated January 2026. This information is for education only and does not replace individual medical advice.

Why vaccines matter

Vaccines prevent serious infections and reduce complications. They are especially important for children with chronic conditions.

Common concerns

  • Mild fever or soreness is normal after many vaccines.
  • Severe allergic reactions are rare; watch in the first hours.

Special note

  • If your child has IBD, liver disease, or is on immune-suppressing medicines, discuss vaccine timing with your specialist.
