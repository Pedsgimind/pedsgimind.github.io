Allergies and allergic rhinitis

Patient & family education. Updated January 2026. This information is for education only and does not replace individual medical advice.

Overview

Nasal allergies cause sneezing, itchy eyes, and congestion, often seasonally.

Helpful steps

  • Trigger reduction (dust, pollen, pets if sensitive).
  • Nasal saline and clinician-recommended medicines.

Seek care if

  • Wheezing or breathing difficulty.
  • Recurrent sinus/ear infections or poor sleep from congestion.
