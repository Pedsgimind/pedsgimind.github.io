Bedwetting (nocturnal enuresis)

Patient & family education. Updated January 2026. This information is for education only and does not replace individual medical advice.

Overview

Bedwetting is common and often resolves with time. It is not a child’s fault.

Helpful strategies

  • Limit evening fluids and caffeine.
  • Bathroom before bed.
  • Bedwetting alarm in motivated families.

When to evaluate further

  • New bedwetting after being dry for months.
  • Daytime symptoms, pain with urination, constipation, or snoring.
