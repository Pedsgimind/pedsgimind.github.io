Ear infections (otitis media)

Patient & family education. Updated January 2026. This information is for education only and does not replace individual medical advice.

Overview

Middle ear infections often occur after a cold. Some children improve without antibiotics, especially if symptoms are mild.

Common symptoms

  • Ear pain, fussiness, trouble sleeping.
  • Fever.
  • Hearing changes temporarily.

Home care

  • Pain control is most important (acetaminophen/ibuprofen if appropriate).
  • Follow your clinician’s plan about antibiotics and follow-up.

Urgent signs

  • Severe pain, swelling behind the ear, ear protruding.
  • Neck stiffness, severe headache, or persistent vomiting.
